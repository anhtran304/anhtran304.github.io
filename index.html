MealPass - UHACK

July - 2018
University of Tasmania

Team leader of Swinny team awarded "The best pitch in Hobart" in UHACK, a Hackathon hosted by University of Tasmania only in 48 hours

MealPass is a platform that comprises of two main parts:

  • A web app for restaurants owners to list food/meal serves that they would not be able to sell by the end of their day, and can list them using a quick and friendly interface to find potentials consumers. They will receive payment from MealPass based on their user rating and how much food they sold through MealPass

  • A mobile app for users to find food listed by restaurants in their vicinity. Users will pay a monthly subscription fee and receive Passes that they can use to exchange for these meals

Check it out: mealpass.info

Motorola Solution Hackathon

May - 2018
Melbourne, Australia

Part of Swinburne University Team, 'The Razorbacks' won Motorola Solutions 2018 Public Safety Hackathon. The operational challenge was provided by the Victoria State Emergency Service (VICSES), one of four public safety organisations participating in the event

Our team came up with a solution including two parts:

  • A mobile prototype to enable citizens to use their smart phones to capture images of fallen trees and other obstacles and leverages other software tools to accurately measure the size of hazards
